# 3D Model Credits

Dragon models used in the portfolio hero scene. All were downloaded from
[Poly Pizza](https://poly.pizza) (the community archive of Google Poly + creator
uploads), which exposes direct, login-free `.glb` download links.

---

## dragon-quaternius.glb  — PRIMARY / recommended

- **Model:** "Dragon" (Ultimate Monsters pack)
- **Author:** Quaternius
- **License:** CC0 1.0 (Public Domain) — no attribution required
- **Source page:** https://poly.pizza/m/VBvzjFIYws
- **Direct download:** https://static.poly.pizza/9714f533-5d2d-4cfd-b8f1-c8dfff64a672.glb
- **Author site:** https://quaternius.com/
- **Size:** ~262 KB
- **Animations:** Yes — `Dragon_Flying`, `Dragon_Attack`, `Dragon_Attack2`, `Dragon_Hit`, `Dragon_Death`
- **Notes:** Winged quadruped dragon with per-part materials (`Wings`, `Belly`,
  `Claws`, `Main`, `Eyes`) — ideal for retinting to a black Night-Fury look while
  keeping a subtle belly accent. Default color is already near-black dark red.
  No textures (flat material colors), so tinting is trivial in three.js.

Attribution (optional, CC0): "Dragon" by Quaternius (https://quaternius.com/) — CC0.

## dragon-evolved-quaternius.glb  — alternate (best idle-hover animation)

- **Model:** "Dragon Evolved"
- **Author:** Quaternius
- **License:** CC0 1.0 (Public Domain) — no attribution required
- **Source page:** https://poly.pizza/m/LlwD0QNUPj
- **Direct download:** https://static.poly.pizza/90ed3740-d8c4-4910-88ce-ac2ed426022d.glb
- **Author site:** https://quaternius.com/
- **Size:** ~426 KB
- **Animations:** Yes — `Flying_Idle`, `Fast_Flying`, `Headbutt`, `HitReact`,
  `Death`, `Punch`, `Yes`, `No` (8 total)
- **Notes:** More detailed rig (61 nodes). `Flying_Idle` is a gentle hover loop —
  arguably the best single clip for a static hero. Materials: `Dragon_Main`,
  `Dragon_Secondary`, `Dragon_Horn`, `Eye_White`, `Eye_Black`. No dedicated wing
  material, but all near-dark and easily tinted. No textures.

Attribution (optional, CC0): "Dragon Evolved" by Quaternius (https://quaternius.com/) — CC0.
